About Us

We want to share our love for the cinema with all the people.

We believe that cinema should be accessible to all. Our concept transforms ordinary public spaces into vibrant outdoor theaters, creating a shared experience that transcends traditional boundaries. From bustling city squares to peaceful parks, we bring cinema to the people, fostering a sense of community and celebration.

Competition

This call applies to film enthusiasts and filmmakers who do not have formal financing to distribute their films. We want short films not longer than 40 minutes made by amateur filmmakers from around the world.

All the selected films will be screened in various cities in North America or Europe. Finally just two films will be awarded. One will be selected by our jury and other will be selected by the audiences. Each price includes:

  • Roadside Prix
  • Winner's certificate
  • Special public screening event
  • Box with several promotional products
Amateur Films related to Animation, Collage Film, Documentary, Experimental Films, Found Footage, New Media, Video Art and Video Poetry.
Advertising Films, Marketing Films, Music Videos, Promotional, Institutional or Commercial Films made by professionals related to the Film Industry.
DV Recordings, Glicht Aesthetics, Hi8 Videos, Home Movies, Super 8 Films, VHS Videos and Short Films made with a Smartphone.
Explicit Sexuality, Gore, Hate Speech, Excessive Violence, Sadism or any content that may offend the audience's sensitivity.
